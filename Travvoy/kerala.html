Explore Kerala: A Culinary
and Cultural Journey

  • Grandmother's Kitchen
    Fort Kochi

    Indulge in authentic Kerala cuisine at Grandmother's Kitchen, where age-old recipes are lovingly prepared to perfection. From succulent seafood to aromatic vegetarian dishes, every bite tells a story of tradition and flavor.

  • Paragon Restaurant
    Calicut

    Embark on a gastronomic journey at Paragon Restaurant, renowned for its Malabar cuisine. Delight your taste buds with aromatic biryanis, fiery curries, and fresh seafood dishes that showcase the unique flavors of Kerala.

  • Dhe Puttu
    Kochi

    Experience a culinary innovation at Dhe Puttu, where the traditional Kerala breakfast dish, puttu, takes center stage. With a variety of sweet and savory options, this quaint eatery offers a delightful twist on a classic favorite.

  • Velleeswaran's Nadan Chayakada
    Alappuzha

    Immerse yourself in the charm of Kerala's tea stalls at Velleeswaran's Nadan Chayakada. Savor piping hot chai paired with freshly prepared snacks like pazhampori and vada, served with a side of authentic local flavor.

  • Malabar Cafe
    Trivandrum

    Discover the culinary treasures of Malabar at Malabar Cafe, located in the heart of Trivandrum. From fluffy appams to aromatic biryanis, each dish is a celebration of Kerala's culinary heritage.

Top 5 Festivals
in Kerala

  • Onam

    Experience the magic of Onam, Kerala's most iconic festival, celebrated in honor of King Mahabali. From vibrant cultural performances to elaborate flower decorations and thrilling boat races, Onam is a celebration of unity, tradition, and joy.

  • Thrissur Pooram

    Witness the grandeur of Thrissur Pooram, a spectacular temple festival known for its mesmerizing procession of decorated elephants, traditional percussion performances, and dazzling fireworks displays.

  • Attukal Pongala

    Join in the festivities of Attukal Pongala, a unique festival dedicated to the Hindu goddess Bhagavathy. Witness thousands of women come together to prepare the sacred offering of Pongala, creating a colorful tapestry of devotion and community spirit.

  • Theyyam

    Immerse yourself in the mystical world of Theyyam, a vibrant ritualistic art form indigenous to North Kerala. Marvel at the intricate costumes and makeup as performers enact stories from Hindu mythology, embodying various deities.

  • Aranmula Boat Race

    Experience the thrill of the Aranmula Boat Race, a traditional snake boat race held during the Onam festival season. Watch skilled oarsmen navigate the waters of the Pampa River, showcasing Kerala's rich maritime heritage and sporting prowess.

  • Padmanabhaswamy Temple
    Thiruvananthapuram

    Discover the architectural marvels of Padmanabhaswamy Temple, a sacred Hindu shrine dedicated to Lord Vishnu. Admire the intricate Dravidian architecture, towering gopurams, and golden embellishments that adorn this ancient temple, showcasing the craftsmanship and devotion of Kerala's artisans.

  • Sree Vadakkunnathan Temple
    Thrissur

    Explore the timeless beauty of Sree Vadakkunnathan Temple, renowned for its classic Kerala architectural style. Marvel at the exquisite wooden carvings, vibrant murals, and traditional koothambalam within the temple complex, reflecting Kerala's rich cultural heritage and spiritual legacy.

  • Guruvayur Temple
    Guruvayur

    Experience the divine aura of Guruvayur Temple, a revered pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Krishna. Admire the grandeur of its entrance, intricate sculptures, and sacred ambiance, which attracts devotees from far and wide seeking blessings and spiritual solace.

  • Chottanikkara Temple
    Kochi

    Delve into the spiritual ambiance of Chottanikkara Temple, dedicated to Goddess Bhagavathy. Witness the unique Kerala temple architecture, vibrant murals, and sacred shrines dedicated to various deities within the temple premises. A place of devotion and reverence that exemplifies Kerala's rich religious heritage.

  • Ambalapuzha Sri Krishna Temple
    Ambalapuzha

    Uncover the architectural splendor of Ambalapuzha Sri Krishna Temple, renowned for its traditional Kerala style architecture and exquisite mural paintings depicting the life of Lord Krishna. Don't miss the opportunity to savor the delicious palpayasam offered as prasadam, adding a culinary delight to your spiritual journey.

Top 5 Activities
in Kerala

  • Houseboat Cruise in
    Alleppey Backwaters

    Immerse yourself in the tranquil beauty of Kerala's backwaters with a houseboat cruise in Alleppey. This iconic experience allows you to glide along palm-fringed canals, serene lagoons, and quaint villages while luxuriating in the comforts of a traditional houseboat. Perfect for relaxation and rejuvenation amidst nature's splendor.

  • Explore Periyar
    Wildlife Sanctuary

    Embark on an adventurous journey through the verdant jungles of Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. Take a memorable boat safari on Periyar Lake, where you can encounter majestic elephants, elusive tigers, colorful birds, and other fascinating wildlife species in their natural habitat. A thrilling wildlife experience awaits amidst the lush greenery of Kerala's wilderness.

  • Trekking in
    Munnar Tea Plantations

    Discover the breathtaking beauty of Munnar's tea plantations through exhilarating trekking trails. As you traverse the mist-covered mountains and cascading waterfalls, you'll be surrounded by lush greenery and panoramic vistas. Trekking in Munnar offers a perfect blend of adventure, serenity, and natural splendor for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

  • Kathakali Performance
    in Kochi

    Immerse yourself in Kerala's rich cultural heritage with a mesmerizing Kathakali performance in Kochi. Witness the intricate makeup, elaborate costumes, and expressive storytelling of this traditional dance-drama, which brings ancient tales and mythology to life on stage. A captivating cultural experience that showcases the artistry and tradition of Kerala's performing arts.

  • Ayurvedic Massage and
    Wellness Retreat

    Rejuvenate your mind, body, and soul with an authentic Ayurvedic massage and wellness retreat in Kerala. Indulge in therapeutic treatments, yoga sessions, and meditation practices amidst the serene surroundings of Ayurvedic resorts. Experience holistic healing and relaxation as you immerse yourself in Kerala's ancient wellness traditions.
