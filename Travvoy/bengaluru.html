Explore Bangalore : A Culinary
and Cultural Journey

  • Mavalli Tiffin Room

    Experience the timeless charm of MTR, an iconic establishment serving authentic South Indian vegetarian cuisine since 1924. Indulge in their legendary Masala Dosa, fluffy Rava Idli, and aromatic Filter Coffee, crafted from cherished recipes passed down through generations.

  • Vidyarthi Bhavan

    Step into the heart of Basavanagudi and discover Vidyarthi Bhavan, renowned for its crispy Benne Masala Dosa, a golden delight that has captivated locals and tourists alike for decades. Join the bustling crowd and savor the flavors of tradition at this beloved eatery.

  • Brahmin's Coffee Bar

    Start your day on a delicious note at Brahmin's Coffee Bar, where soft idlis and steaming filter coffee await. Delight in the simplicity of their offerings and relish the authentic flavors of South Indian breakfast cuisine

  • Truffles

    Treat yourself to a symphony of flavors at Truffles, a cozy café renowned for its indulgent burgers, cheesy fries, and decadent desserts. Unwind in the warm ambiance and savor every bite of comfort food heaven.

  • Nagarjuna

    Embark on a culinary odyssey to Andhra Pradesh at Nagarjuna, where fiery spices and bold flavors reign supreme. Indulge in their aromatic biryanis, tantalizing curries, and exquisite seafood dishes that pay homage to the rich culinary heritage of the region.

Top 5 Festivals
in Bangalore

  • Ugadi

    Ugadi, the Kannada New Year, heralds the start of a fresh lunar cycle with boundless enthusiasm and zest in Bangalore. Families gather to perform traditional rituals, exchange heartfelt greetings, and indulge in sumptuous feasts. The city adorns itself in vibrant hues, with streets adorned in intricate rangoli designs and alive with cultural performances, making Ugadi a joyous occasion brimming with hope, prosperity, and new beginnings.

  • Dussehra

    Dussehra, also known as Vijayadashami, unfolds in grandeur and splendour across Bangalore, commemorating the victory of righteousness over darkness. Magnificent processions traverse the city streets, showcasing towering effigies of the demon king Ravana that culminate in spectacular displays of fire and fervor. Traditional music, dance performances, and captivating dramas add to the festive fervor, uniting communities in the spirit of triumph and renewal.

  • Diwali

    Diwali, the radiant festival of lights, illuminates Bangalore with a dazzling spectacle of lamps, fireworks, and ornate decorations. Homes, temples, and markets come alive with twinkling lights and vibrant rangoli patterns, creating an enchanting ambiance of joy and prosperity. Families exchange sweets, gifts, and warm wishes, while the city resonates with the jubilant spirit of Diwali, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness and the triumph of good over evil.

  • Karaga Festival

    The Karaga Festival, steeped in ancient mystique, unfolds as a unique cultural spectacle in Bangalore, honoring the revered goddess Draupadi. The city reverberates with the rhythmic beats of drums and chants as devotees partake in the mesmerizing Karaga procession. Led by a priest embodying Draupadi, adorned with ornate regalia and bearing a sacred pot atop his head, the procession weaves through the streets in a mesmerizing display of devotion and tradition, captivating onlookers and believers alike.

  • Bangalore Habba

    Bangalore Habba, the city's signature Arts Festival, is a vibrant celebration of creativity, diversity, and cultural expression that captivates audiences year after year. Organized by the Karnataka government, the festival showcases a dazzling array of artistic endeavours, including music concerts, dance performances, theatre productions, art exhibitions, and literary gatherings. Bangalore Habba serves as a melting pot of cultural exchange, fostering a sense of community and camaraderie among Bangaloreans and visitors alike, while showcasing the city's rich cultural tapestry to the world.

  • Bangalore Fort
    Heritage Walk

    Step back in time with a guided heritage walk through Bangalore Fort, where ancient temples, majestic palaces, and historic monuments narrate tales of the city's rich cultural heritage. Immerse yourself in history and uncover the secrets of Bangalore's royal past.

  • Lalbagh Botanical
    Garden Retreat

    Escape the urban hustle and bustle at Lalbagh Botanical Garden, a serene oasis amidst the city's chaos. Explore lush greenery, fragrant flower beds, and tranquil lakes while discovering rare plant species and experiencing the calming embrace of nature.

  • Karnataka Chitrakala
    Parishath Art Appreciation

    Immerse yourself in Bangalore's vibrant art scene at Karnataka Chitrakala Parishath, home to a diverse collection of paintings, sculptures, and contemporary artworks. Delve into the world of art exhibitions and cultural events, enriching your cultural experience in Silicon Valley.

  • Commercial Street
    Silk Saree Shopping

    Indulge in a shopping spree at Commercial Street, Bangalore's bustling shopping district renowned for its vibrant markets and eclectic boutiques. Browse through exquisite silk sarees, traditional handicrafts, and ethnic jewelry, capturing the essence of Bangalore's rich cultural heritage.

  • Bannerghatta National Park
    Nature Walk

    Embark on a scenic nature walk amidst the pristine wilderness of Bannerghatta National Park, a sanctuary teeming with diverse flora and fauna. Encounter majestic wildlife, breathe in the fresh air, and reconnect with nature in this picturesque natural paradise.

Top 5 Activities
in Bangalore

  • VR Gaming Thrills at VR World

    Dive into the future of entertainment at VR World, Bangalore's premier virtual reality gaming arcade. With adrenaline-pumping games and immersive experiences, VR World guarantees an adventure that will leave you exhilarated and wanting more.

  • MG Road Street Art Stroll

    Embark on a vibrant exploration of MG Road's street art scene, where colorful murals and captivating graffiti adorn the city's walls. Capture Insta-worthy shots of Bangalore's urban culture and immerse yourself in the creativity that defines this bustling district.

  • Indiranagar Nightlife Excursion

    Experience Bangalore's nightlife hotspot, Indiranagar, where trendy cafes, chic bars, and lively pubs create an electrifying atmosphere. Join the party-goers and revel in the music, drinks, and vibrant energy that make Indiranagar a must-visit destination.

  • Wonderla Adventure Park

    Seeking an adrenaline rush? Look no further than Wonderla, Bangalore's ultimate amusement park destination. From gravity-defying rides to thrilling water slides, Wonderla promises a day of non-stop fun and excitement for thrill-seeking adventurers.

  • Church Street Food Truck Festival

    Satiate your culinary cravings at the Church Street Food Truck Festival, a gastronomic extravaganza featuring a mouth-watering array of street food delights. Sample diverse flavors from around the world and indulge in the culinary delights that define Bangalore's vibrant food scene.
