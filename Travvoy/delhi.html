Explore Delhi : A Culinary
and Cultural Journey

  • Chandni Chowk

    Step into the historic lanes of Chandni Chowk, where every corner is a feast for the senses. Indulge in the iconic stuffed parathas at Paranthe Wali Gali or savor the tangy chaats at Natraj Bhalla Corner. With a myriad of flavors and aromas, Chandni Chowk promises an unforgettable street food experience.

  • Karim's

    Located near Jama Masjid, Karim's is a culinary institution renowned for its delectable Mughlai cuisine. From succulent kebabs to aromatic biryanis, every dish at Karim's is a testament to the rich culinary heritage of Delhi.

  • Indian Accent

    For a contemporary twist on Indian cuisine, head to Indian Accent in The Lodhi hotel. Experience a culinary journey like no other with innovative dishes crafted from traditional ingredients, leaving your taste buds craving for more.

  • Dilli Haat

    Explore the flavors of India under one roof at Dilli Haat, an open-air market showcasing cuisines from different states. From Rajasthani dal baati churma to Bengali fish curry, Dilli Haat promises a gastronomic adventure for every palate.

  • Pandara Road

    Pandara Road is a culinary hotspot known for its array of restaurants serving authentic North Indian cuisine. Indulge in classics like butter chicken and kebabs amidst a lively atmosphere that captures the essence of Delhi's food culture.

Top 5 Festivals
in Delhi

  • Qutub Festival

    Experience the magic of classical music and dance against the backdrop of the majestic Qutub Minar at the annual Qutub Festival. Hosted by the Delhi Tourism and Transportation Development Corporation, this event draws acclaimed artists from across India, creating an enchanting atmosphere that mesmerizes visitors.

  • International Mango Festival

    Delight in the sweetness of summer at Delhi's International Mango Festival, a celebration dedicated to the beloved mango. Organized by the Delhi Tourism department, this festival offers a tantalizing array of mango varieties from India and around the globe, accompanied by tasting sessions, culinary demonstrations, and cultural performances.

  • Sufi Sangeet Festival

    Immerse yourself in the mystical world of Sufism at Delhi's Sufi Sangeet Festival, where soul-stirring music performances transport you to a realm of love, peace, and spiritual harmony. Held at various venues across the city, this festival brings together renowned Sufi singers and musicians for enchanting evenings of devotional renditions.

  • Delhi Comic Con

    Join the vibrant community of comic book enthusiasts, cosplayers, and artists at Delhi Comic Con, an annual extravaganza of all things geeky and fun. Hosted by Comic Con India, this event features engaging panels, creative workshops, merchandise stalls, cosplay showcases, and celebrity interactions, making it a must-visit for fans of comics, anime, and gaming.

  • Dastkar Nature Bazaar

    Explore the vibrant world of traditional Indian crafts and sustainable living practices at the Dastkar Nature Bazaar, an annual event held at Delhi's Dilli Haat. Discover exquisite handmade products crafted by artisans from rural and tribal communities, including textiles, pottery, jewelry, and organic foods, while enjoying cultural performances and enlightening workshops on sustainable living.

  • Explore Heritage Sites

    Delve into Delhi's rich history by visiting iconic landmarks like Humayun's Tomb and Qutub Minar. These UNESCO World Heritage sites offer a serene atmosphere and picturesque surroundings, perfect for leisurely exploration and appreciation of architectural marvels.

  • Indulge in Culinary Delights

    Embark on a culinary journey through the bustling streets of Old Delhi with a guided food tour. From savory Mughlai dishes to spicy chaats, you'll savor the authentic flavors of Delhi's street food scene, tantalizing your taste buds along the way

  • Discover Tranquility in Hauz Khas Village

    Escape the hustle and bustle of the city with a leisurely stroll through Hauz Khas Village. Explore its historic landmarks, boutique shops, and art galleries, and unwind amidst the tranquil surroundings of this charming neighborhood.

  • Shop for Handicrafts

    Browse through the vibrant stalls at Dilli Haat and discover exquisite handicrafts crafted by traditional Indian artisans. From textiles to pottery, you'll find unique souvenirs and gifts to commemorate your Delhi experience.

  • Relax in Lodi Gardens

    Reconnect with nature at Lodi Gardens, a sprawling park dotted with historic monuments and lush greenery. Take a leisurely walk along scenic trails, admire the architectural marvels, and unwind amidst the serene ambiance of this urban oasis.

Top 5 Activities
in Delhi

  • Thrilling Rickshaw Ride in Old Delhi

    Dive into the vibrant atmosphere of Old Delhi with an exhilarating rickshaw ride through its narrow lanes. Experience the city's rich history and bustling street life up close, making unforgettable memories along the way.

  • Experience the Vibrant Nightlife

    Immerse yourself in Delhi's electrifying nightlife scene at Connaught Place, where trendy bars, pubs, and clubs come alive after dark. Socialize, dance, and soak in the energetic vibe of the city's most happening hub.

  • Catch a Bollywood Movie Screening

    Immerse yourself in India's colorful cinematic culture with a Bollywood movie screening at a local theater in Delhi. Experience the magic of Hindi cinema firsthand and enjoy an entertaining evening filled with music, drama, and dance.

  • Shop for Trendy Fashion
    in Hauz Khas

    Indulge in a shopping spree at Hauz Khas Village, home to fashionable boutiques and designer stores offering the latest trends in clothing and accessories. Discover unique fashion finds and express your personal style in this hip neighborhood.

  • Attend a Cultural Performance

    Immerse yourself in Delhi's vibrant cultural scene with a visit to the India Habitat Centre, where you can attend cultural performances ranging from music concerts to dance recitals. Experience the diversity and dynamism of Indian arts and entertainment firsthand.
