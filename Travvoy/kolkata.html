Explore Kolkata : A Culinary
and Cultural Journey

  • Mocambo
    Park Street

    Another gem on Park Street, Mocambo is celebrated for its Continental and Indian dishes. The deviled crabs and sizzlers are must-try items, complemented by the restaurant's timeless ambiance.

  • Peter Cat
    Park Street

    For a taste of Kolkata's colonial legacy, head to Peter Cat on Park Street. Renowned for its Chelo Kebabs, this iconic restaurant offers a delightful fusion of Indian and continental cuisines in a classic, old-world setting.

  • Arsalan
    Park Circus

    If you're a fan of Mughlai cuisine, Arsalan in Park Circus is the place to be. Renowned for its delectable biryanis and kebabs, this restaurant captures the essence of Kolkata's rich culinary diversity.

  • Indian Coffee House
    College Street

    Nestled in the historic College Street, the Indian Coffee House is a cultural institution known for its intellectual ambiance and authentic Bengali snacks. Enjoy a cup of strong coffee paired with their famous cutlets or adda (informal conversations) with locals.

Top 5 Festivals
in Kolkata

  • Durga Puja

    Experience unparalleled zeal during Navaratri with Durga Puja, a festival that transforms the city into a breathtaking canvas of creativity. Explore dazzling pandals, artistic idols, and cultural performances that make this celebration a must-visit for locals and tourists alike.

  • Kali Puja Celebration

    Dive into the vibrant energy and cultural richness of Kali Puja, a celebration dedicated to the formidable Goddess Kali. Discover how the city sparkles with lights and artistic representations, adding a unique charm to Kolkata's festive calendar.

  • Saraswati Puja Extravaganza

    Join the fervor of Saraswati Puja, an event that honors the Goddess of wisdom, knowledge, and arts. Explore beautifully crafted idols in educational institutions, homes, and public spaces, creating an atmosphere of learning and creativity.

  • Jagadhatri Puja's
    Artistic Flair

    Witness the elaborate processions and cultural events of Jagadhatri Puja, dedicated to the goddess Jagadhatri, an incarnation of Durga. Uncover the unique artistic expressions that add a distinctive flavor to the city's festive tapestry.

  • Victoria Memorial and
    Maidan Exploration

    Visit the iconic Victoria Memorial, a symbol of Kolkata's colonial heritage. Stroll through the lush greenery of the Maidan, a vast urban park adjacent to the memorial. The serene surroundings make it an ideal spot for relaxation and a glimpse into the city's past.

  • Tram Ride through
    Esplanade

    Embark on a nostalgic tram ride through Esplanade, Kolkata's bustling city center. The slow-paced journey allows you to soak in the city's vibrant atmosphere, passing by historic buildings, markets, and cultural hubs. It's a delightful way to experience Kolkata's charm.

  • Interactive Art Installations
    at Science City

    Visit Science City for a blend of education and entertainment. Engage with interactive art installations, 3D exhibits, and virtual reality experiences that cater to the tech-savvy Gen Z mindset. It's a perfect destination for those seeking a fusion of learning and fun.

Top 5 Activities
in Kolkata

  • Boat Ride on
    the Hooghly River

    Take a leisurely boat ride on the Hooghly River, capturing the essence of Kolkata's riverfront. Enjoy stunning views of the Howrah Bridge and iconic ghats. The boat ride provides a unique perspective on the city's skyline, offering a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle.

  • Coffee Shop Hopping
    in South Kolkata

    South Kolkata boasts a thriving coffee culture with numerous quirky and indie coffee shops. Spend a day coffee shop hopping, indulging in artisanal brews, and capturing the cozy aesthetics that resonate with the Gen Z penchant for aesthetically pleasing spaces.

  • Food Truck Exploration
    in Salt Lake

    Head to Salt Lake, an area known for its emerging food truck culture. Try a variety of innovative and fusion street foods, showcasing Kolkata's culinary creativity. It's an Instagrammable experience that caters to the Gen Z love for food exploration.

  • Street Art and Graffiti Hunt
    in Bow Barracks

    Discover the vibrant street art scene in Bow Barracks, where colorful murals adorn the walls. Engage in a street art and graffiti hunt, capturing Instagram-worthy shots while immersing yourself in the contemporary art scene of Kolkata.
